Our Story
  • 09/2006 Boy meets girl on her first day of college. (Girl doesn't remember this event.)
  • 01/2007 Boy and girl meet again when they volunteer at a local hospice every week. (Girl remembers this.)
  • 10/2007 Boy asks girl out. Girl does not realize this is a date and brings her friend.
  • 11/2007 Exasperated boy asks girl out again, this time as explicitly as possible. Girl understands. They have their first official date over sushi.
  • 12/2007 Several dates later, boy surprises girl with an ice cream study break. Girl is pleased. Relationship continues.
  • 09/2008 Boy and girl take a ballroom dance class. They discover they are terrible. (Not an important timepoint. Just want to lower expectations for the wedding dance.)
  • 06/2009 Boy graduates from college and leaves for medical school. Thus begin the 6-hour weekend trips between New Haven and Philadelphia.
  • 06/2010 Girl graduates from college and starts medical school in New York. Two hour bus rides to Philly are a huge improvement.
  • 12/2013 After 5 years of long distance, boy and girl travel to countless cities across the U.S. for residency interviews, in hopes of matching together.
  • 06/2014 At long last, they match together in California!
    They move in together and embark on crazy adventures.
  • November 7th, 2015 Boy proposes to girl after 8 years together. Girl accepts.
  • July 22nd, 2017 Boy and girl marry, and they celebrate with their many friends and families who were part of their long, long journey.
